Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie

Fast, no bake, simple, clean, protein packed, and addicting

What You'll Need

  • Pie Crust
  • 1 Jar of Peanut Butter
  • 1 Bag of Vegan Chocolate Chips
  • 1 Package of Silken Tofu
  • Your choice of Vegan milk

Steps

  1. Using a large mixing bowl, dump desired amount of choclate chips in, the more the sweeter!
  2. Place in a microwave for 30 seconds to 1 miunte and mix until smooth
  3. Add Peanut Butter, the more the thicker the pie will be
  4. Add some Vegan milk, not too much or it will be too soft
  5. Mix together until smooth
  6. pour mixture into crust
  7. Place in fridge for 12 to 24hrs!
  8. Enjoy.
