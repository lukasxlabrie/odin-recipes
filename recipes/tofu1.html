GENERAL TSO TOFU

Brigtly colored, Spicy-sweet, protein-packed, and super easy to cook!

What you'll need:

  • Extra Firm Tofu
  • Low Sodium Soy Sauce
  • General Tso sauce
  • Garlic Powder
  • Sea Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Cornstarch
  • Sesame Oil
  • Frozen Brocolli
  • Jasmine Rice
  • Red Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Coat the tofu. Place the cubed tofu in a medium bowl. Add the liquid aminos and toss to coat. Add the garlic powder, sea salt, black pepper, and cornstarch; toss again, making sure the tofu is evenly coated.
  2. Cook the tofu. Heat a pan or wok over medium-high heat, and add the oil. When hot, add the tofu and cook on all sides until golden and crispy, about 8 minutes total.
  3. Cook Rice
  4. Add Brocolli to tofu
  5. Add remaining ingredients to taste
